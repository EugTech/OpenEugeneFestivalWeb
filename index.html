What is Open Eugene Festival?

You’ll see Mozilla’s ethos of “open” in action at the festival – it’s about making concepts and projects

  • 1) easy to understand,
  • 2) easy to share, remix and build on, and
  • 3) welcoming to all participants, from any background.
Open Eugene Festival is a great space to experience and experiment with this approach
—we call it “working open”

The Festival is part hack-a-thon, part conference, part art exhibition. We are working with learners & leaders to help citizens of Eugene open projects, improve collaboration, and inspire the community.

When and Where

Open Eugene Fest will be happening Friday-Saturday December 14th & 15th 2018 at Trifoia.
